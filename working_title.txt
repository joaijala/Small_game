Game story (may not be seen in the final wersion):

The player has stolen a treasure from the monsters. Now his/hers task is to protect the treasure from the monsters that want it back.



Core gameplay:
The game will be a kind of fps capture the flag game.
At the beginning of the game, player is given a treasure.
The player has to deploy the treasure somewhere in the map.
After this the treasure cant be moved. 
After this monsters will spawn and trie to steal the treasure. The player has to protect the treasure.
The game will end when the player dies or if the whole treasure is stolen.

Enemies target mainly the treasure, but they do attack if player is nearby or are attacked by the player.
The terrain will have different places in the terrain that is good for defence in different way. 



Special features (if there's time to implement):
Enemies can come in waves.
Enemies may spawn randomly from different directions on each wave.
Enemies with different skillsets may spawn on different waves.

Multiplayer co-op.

The treasure can be moved later
